# AS-IS Process (Current Recruitment Workflow)

The AS-IS process represents the current manual recruitment workflow used in most organizations before AI implementation.

---

## Process Flow

1. Job requisition is created by Hiring Manager
2. Job is posted on job portals (LinkedIn, company website, etc.)
3. Candidates submit CVs manually
4. Recruiters manually review each CV
5. Recruiters shortlist candidates based on subjective evaluation
6. Shortlisted candidates are forwarded to Hiring Managers
7. Interviews are scheduled manually
8. Final selection is made

---

## Key Issues in AS-IS Process

- High manual workload for recruiters
- Inconsistent evaluation criteria
- Delayed screening and shortlisting
- Human bias in candidate selection
- Poor scalability for high-volume hiring
