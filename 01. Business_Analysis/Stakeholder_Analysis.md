# Stakeholder Analysis

This section identifies and analyzes the key stakeholders involved in the AI Recruitment Screening System and their roles, interests, and influence on the system.

---

## Key Stakeholders

### 1. HR Managers
- **Role:** Oversee recruitment strategy and workforce planning
- **Interest:** Efficient hiring process, reduced time-to-hire
- **Influence:** High
- **Impact:** Defines recruitment policies and approves system adoption

---

### 2. Recruiters
- **Role:** Primary users of the system
- **Interest:** Fast and accurate candidate screening
- **Influence:** High
- **Impact:** Daily system usage and feedback

---

### 3. Hiring Managers
- **Role:** Define job requirements and approve final candidates
- **Interest:** High-quality candidates aligned with job needs
- **Influence:** High
- **Impact:** Validate AI-generated shortlists

---

### 4. Candidates (Applicants)
- **Role:** Submit applications and CVs
- **Interest:** Fair evaluation and timely feedback
- **Influence:** Low
- **Impact:** End users affected by screening outcomes

---

### 5. AI/IT Team
- **Role:** Develop and maintain the system
- **Interest:** System accuracy, scalability, performance
- **Influence:** Medium
- **Impact:** Responsible for technical implementation

---

### 6. Compliance / Legal Team
- **Role:** Ensure legal and ethical compliance
- **Interest:** Data privacy, bias mitigation, fairness
- **Influence:** High
- **Impact:** Defines constraints and governance rules

---

## Power vs Interest Summary

| Stakeholder        | Power | Interest | Strategy            |
|--------------------|-------|----------|---------------------|
| HR Managers        | High  | High     | Manage closely      |
| Recruiters         | High  | High     | Engage continuously |
| Hiring Managers    | High  | High     | Manage closely      |
| Candidates         | Low   | High     | Keep informed       |
| AI/IT Team         | Medium| High     | Support & engage    |
| Compliance Team    | High  | Medium   | Keep satisfied      |
