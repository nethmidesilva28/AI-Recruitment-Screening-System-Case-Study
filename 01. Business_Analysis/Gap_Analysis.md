# Gap Analysis

This section compares the AS-IS and TO-BE recruitment processes to identify improvements introduced by the AI system.

---

## 1. Process Efficiency

- **AS-IS:** Fully manual CV screening
- **TO-BE:** Automated CV parsing and ranking
- **Gap:** Significant reduction in manual effort

---

## 2. Decision Making

- **AS-IS:** Subjective recruiter judgment
- **TO-BE:** AI-assisted standardized scoring
- **Gap:** More consistent and data-driven decisions

---

## 3. Time to Hire

- **AS-IS:** Slow due to manual screening
- **TO-BE:** Faster due to automated filtering
- **Gap:** Reduced recruitment cycle time

---

## 4. Bias and Fairness

- **AS-IS:** High risk of unconscious bias
- **TO-BE:** Bias mitigation algorithms applied
- **Gap:** Improved fairness in screening

---

## 5. Scalability

- **AS-IS:** Limited scalability
- **TO-BE:** Handles high-volume applications efficiently
- **Gap:** Enterprise-level scalability achieved

---

## Summary

The AI Recruitment Screening System bridges critical inefficiencies in traditional recruitment by introducing automation, intelligence, and consistency into the candidate evaluation process.
