# Objectives

The primary objective of this project is to design an AI-powered Recruitment Screening System that enhances the efficiency, fairness, and accuracy of the hiring process.

## Business Objectives
- Reduce CV screening time by automating initial candidate evaluation
- Improve quality of shortlisted candidates
- Standardize candidate assessment across recruiters
- Reduce unconscious bias in early recruitment stages

## System Objectives
- Parse and structure unstructured CV data
- Match candidates to job descriptions using AI-based scoring
- Rank candidates based on job relevance
- Provide recruiter-friendly dashboards for decision-making

## Strategic Objectives
- Enable scalable recruitment for high-volume hiring environments
- Support data-driven hiring decisions
- Improve overall candidate experience through faster feedback cycles
