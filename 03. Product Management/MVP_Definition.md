# MVP Definition

---

## Objective of MVP

Deliver a minimum viable product that automates CV screening and ranking to reduce manual recruiter effort.

---

## Core Features (MVP Scope)

### 1. CV Upload & Parsing
- Upload CV (PDF/DOCX)
- Extract skills, experience, education

---

### 2. Job Description Processing
- Input job description
- Extract required skills and qualifications

---

### 3. AI Matching Engine
- Compare CV data with job requirements
- Generate relevance score

---

### 4. Candidate Ranking
- Rank candidates from highest to lowest score

---

### 5. Basic Recruiter Dashboard
- View candidate list
- View scores
- Basic filtering

---

## Out of MVP Scope

- Interview scheduling
- Video interview analysis
- Advanced bias detection models
- ATS full integration

---

## Success Criteria

- Reduce screening time by at least 50%
- Successfully rank candidates with acceptable relevance accuracy
