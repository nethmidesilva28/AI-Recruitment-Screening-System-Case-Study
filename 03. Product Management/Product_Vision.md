# Product Vision – AI Recruitment Screening System

---

## Vision Statement

To build an intelligent recruitment screening system that enables organizations to hire faster, smarter, and fairer by leveraging AI-driven candidate evaluation and ranking.

---

## Problem We Solve

Recruitment teams face:
- High volume of CVs
- Slow manual screening processes
- Inconsistent candidate evaluation
- Risk of human bias

---

## Solution Overview

An AI-powered system that:
- Parses CVs into structured data
- Matches candidates with job descriptions
- Ranks candidates based on relevance
- Assists recruiters in decision-making through intelligent dashboards

---

## Value Proposition

- Faster hiring cycles
- Improved candidate quality
- Reduced recruiter workload
- Fair and consistent evaluation process

---

## Target Users

- HR Managers
- Recruiters
- Hiring Managers

---

## Long-Term Vision

To evolve into a full AI-driven recruitment intelligence platform supporting end-to-end hiring optimization.
