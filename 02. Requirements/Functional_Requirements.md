# Functional Requirements

---

## CV Processing
- System shall allow CV upload (PDF, DOCX)
- System shall extract skills, experience, education

---

## Job Processing
- System shall parse job descriptions
- System shall extract required skills and experience

---

## Matching Engine
- System shall match candidate profiles with job requirements
- System shall calculate relevance score

---

## Ranking System
- System shall rank candidates from highest to lowest score
- System shall filter candidates based on threshold score

---

## Recruiter Dashboard
- System shall display ranked candidate list
- System shall allow filtering and sorting
- System shall allow manual override of AI decisions
