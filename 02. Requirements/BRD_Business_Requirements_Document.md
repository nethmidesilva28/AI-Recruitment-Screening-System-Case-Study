# Business Requirements Document (BRD)
## AI Recruitment Screening System

---

## 1. Introduction

This document defines the business requirements for an AI-powered Recruitment Screening System designed to improve efficiency, consistency, and fairness in candidate evaluation.

---

## 2. Business Problem

Organizations face inefficiencies in manual CV screening, leading to:
- Long recruitment cycles
- Inconsistent candidate evaluation
- High recruiter workload
- Unconscious bias in hiring decisions

---

## 3. Business Objectives

- Reduce time spent on CV screening
- Improve quality of shortlisted candidates
- Standardize recruitment evaluation process
- Enhance fairness and reduce bias

---

## 4. Scope

### In Scope
- CV parsing and structuring
- AI-based candidate-job matching
- Candidate ranking system
- Recruiter dashboard

### Out of Scope
- Interview scheduling automation
- Payroll or HR management systems
- Background verification

---

## 5. Stakeholders
- HR Managers
- Recruiters
- Hiring Managers
- Candidates
- Compliance Team

---

## 6. High-Level Requirements

- System shall parse CVs into structured data
- System shall match candidates with job descriptions
- System shall generate ranked candidate lists
- System shall allow recruiter overrides

---

## 7. Business Success Criteria

- 50–70% reduction in screening time
- Improved candidate-job match accuracy
- Reduced manual workload for recruiters
