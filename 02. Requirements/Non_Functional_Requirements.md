# Non-Functional Requirements

---

## Performance
- System shall process each CV within 3 seconds
- System shall support high-volume batch processing

---

## Scalability
- System shall handle thousands of CVs per job posting

---

## Security
- All candidate data shall be encrypted
- Access control shall be role-based

---

## Reliability
- System uptime shall be 99.9%
- System shall recover from failures automatically

---

## Usability
- Dashboard shall be intuitive for non-technical users
- Minimal training required for recruiters

---

## Compliance
- System shall comply with data privacy regulations
- Candidate consent shall be required for data processing
