# Bias Mitigation Strategy

---

## Problem Statement

AI systems in recruitment can unintentionally introduce or amplify bias based on historical hiring data or biased input signals.

---

## Types of Bias Risks

- Gender bias
- Age bias
- Name/ethnicity bias
- Educational institution bias
- Experience bias

---

## Mitigation Strategies

### 1. Feature Removal
- Remove sensitive attributes (name, gender, age) during preprocessing

---

### 2. Standardized Scoring
- Use structured evaluation criteria instead of subjective inputs

---

### 3. Weighted Fairness Model
- Ensure no single attribute dominates scoring

---

### 4. Explainability Layer
- Provide reasoning behind candidate scores

---

### 5. Human-in-the-loop Validation
- Recruiters retain final decision authority

---

## Ethical Principle

The system supports **decision assistance**, not **decision replacement**.

---

## Outcome Goal

- Fairer candidate evaluation
- Reduced unconscious bias
- Transparent AI decision-making process
